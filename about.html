What We Are

We help you thrive in the 21st century.

The Shuford Program in Entrepreneurship, founded in 2004, seeks to foster an entrepreneurial outlook among arts and sciences students with different backgrounds and interests.The program's objective is to train students to approach the world from an entrepreneurial perspective. To this end, our principles are designed to assist students with entrepreneurial aspirations in realizing their diverse startup goals by offering them extensive networks, honing their communication skills, and facilitating collaboration opportunities.

The inaugural Shuford Innovator-in-Residence Program brought together a cohort of industry subject-matter experts with an intentional focus on diversity of personal and professional backgrounds, experiences, and identities. These leaders in innovation all Carolina alumni support students in the Shuford Program in Entrepreneurship through academic lectures, professional development initiatives, mentoring opportunities, and candid conversations across campus. Each innovator visits campus for one week per academic year to build connections and then networks with the University and Shuford to continue to build community beyond that week.

What We Do

We empower our students to turn ideas and inspiration into action by:

  • Understanding the basic principles and underpinnings of a successful start-up whether it be a for-profit, non-profit, new venture or project within a larger organization
  • Developing confidence in communicating, collaborating, and networking
  • Gaining entrepreneurial experience by hearing from, engaging with, and working alongside practicing entrepreneurs.
